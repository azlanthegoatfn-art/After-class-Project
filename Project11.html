This webpage presents a balanced weekly fitness plan with organized workout cards Each section is designed using padding, borders, and margins for a clean and easy-to-read layout.

Treadmill Running

Running on a treadmill is an excellent cardio exercise that improves heart health, burns calories, and increases stamina.

Time: 15 minutes

Bench Press

The bench press is one of the best exercises for building chest, shoulder, and tricep strength.

Time: 10 minutes

Lat Pulldown

The lat pulldown targets your back muscles, especially the lats, while also working your biceps and shoulders.

Time: 10 minutes

Leg Press

The leg press strengthens your quadriceps, hamstrings, glutes, and calves. It allows you to safely lift heavier weights than squats

Time: 10 minutes

Cycling

A light evening bike ride is a fun way to stay active, improve cardiovascular fitness, and enjoy fresh air while burning calories.

Time: 25-30 minutes

Rest and Hydrate

Drink enough water and take proper rest after completing your workout.

Time: All day

Fitness Tip: "Stay consistent with your workouts, eat a balanced diet, drink plenty of water, and get 7–9 hours of sleep each night.
